Buildings data visualized in itself!

I'm experimenting with new ways of providing whole world building data. As I came across several quad tiling approaches, I wanted to visualize how adaptive tiles would look like.

Explanation: data is segmented into for even rectangles, then its counted, whether there are too many polygon points in it, so a quad gets subdivided one further step. And so on.

Very small rectangles - and thus tall pillars - stand for high point density.
